About Us

OASIS AGROVET

Our company "Oasis AgroVet" seeks primarily to support projects in the field of:

Agricultural education.

Protect the environment and reduce the consumption of natural resources.

Scientific research in the field of livestock and poultry.

Educational work aimed at raising awareness about the quality of education in the field of agriculture.

Oasis AgroVet targets to occupy 60% of the Rwandan market. The secret of our success is continuous improvement in service delivery and distribution of high quality products.

Company Full Description

● In 2018, the company Oasis AgroVet was founded by Bazizane Umunyana Fionnah. Began his work with supplying and wholesale of certified variety of products through accredited distributors and a retail network throughout the entire country of Rwanda.

● Oasis AgroVet is among the very few companies in Rwanda (but the leading one) that provides unique integrated solutions in agriculture, animal husbandry and farming, from feeding to business projects on a turnkey basis.

● Oasis AgroVet has grown into a major player in the livestock business, providing comprehensive services in the field of feeding and veterinary medicine.

● Oasis AgroVet is a focused agrovet company, dedicated to improving the productivity of Rwandan farmers by bringing good quality products and services at the Rwanda Marketplace that sustainably increase crop and livestock yields at very affordable prices. We hold leading market positions in the distribution of different products in which we operate – Agriculture inputs, Veterinary medecine,Animal Feed, Dairy and Poultry and Processed Foods.

● Oasis AgroVet supplies agricultural and veterinary commodities through its network of clients who’re the company’s retailers, based in different regions of Rwanda.

● Oasis AgroVet is a one of key players of Rwandan agro-industrial market and a leading distributor/supplier of nutrition components for farm. Oasis AgroVet professionally specializes in veterinary medical supply services and crop production commodities, as well as technological consulting and business processes management at livestock and poultry farms

Company overview

OASIS AGROVET LTD is a wholesale and retail agro-veterinary business. It is built on the idea that leads to agriculture and veterinary product supply, dispensing pharmaceutical products, and quality feed additives such as premixes, amino acids and vitamins.

OASIS AGROVET LTD is also an agriculture and veterinary services provider that assist farmers in their daily farming activities and provides advice to our customers to enhance productivity, identify quality products and services at the right time and in the right place.

Bazizane Umunyana Fionnah

Our customers are our priorities.

The company now provides animal drugs and animal feeds additives and supplements, our customers are individual farmers both men and women especially those in remote and rural areas whose income is mostly based on farming, cooperatives of farmers and animal feeds manufacturers. Our target customers are interested in purchasing high quality products, which boost their production. They value quality and they research the products they buy.

Our Goal: The goal was (and still is) to provide a service where local farms are given priority and members are able to access the freshest organic items available at a fair price.

We’ve spent the past few years building a business and service that we love. A service that makes the safest
